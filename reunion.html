2017 Family Reunion

Welcome to the Family Website Reunion page.

 

Friday Events

 		  

On Friday night we will provide a bus to Foxwoods. Foxwoods is the biggest and best gaming complex in the world with Blackjack, Roulette, Baccarat, High Stakes Bingo, Poker, Keno, Craps, Let it Ride, Caribbean Stud, Ultimate Race Book and over 5,500 exciting Slots. When visiting Foxwoods, please stop at any Wampum Club and apply for your FREE personal Wampum card.

 

Saturday Events

 

Saturday we will have the annual picnic in the park. Meals, Activities and Entertainment. The complete picnic menu begins at 12:00 noon and we will leave the park at 6:00 pm. We will have active activities for the young and young at heart & not-so-active activities for those interested in really concentrating on relaxing. There will be a variety of entertainment for younger kids, older kids, adults, and entire families.

Our Menu for the day will be as follows:

  • Barbecued Spring Chicken
  • Baked Ham with a Brandied Fruit Sauce
  • Fried Chicken
  • Sliced Barbecued Pork
  • Shells with Marinara Sauce & Cheese
  • Spanish Rice
  • Corn
  • Potato Salad
  • Toss Salad
  • Fresh Rolls & Butter
  • Brownies & Ice Cream
